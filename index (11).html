Our Mission
H4U (HomeOwnership4U) exists to close the homeownership gap for Utah County working families. We partner directly with cities to apply a proven, data-driven playbook that removes unnecessary cost from the development process -- so a family earning $100K--$150K can actually afford to buy a home in the community where they work.

Key Terms Explained
PITI
Principal + Interest + Taxes + Insurance -- the total monthly housing payment lenders use to qualify borrowers.
DTI
Debt-to-Income Ratio -- the percentage of gross monthly income toward housing. FHA front-end guideline is 31%.
Traditional Price
The all-in cost to build a home using current market practices -- land, fees, construction, financing, profit, and marketing.
H4U Net Price
The reduced home price after applying H4U Playbook levers. This is what a working family would actually pay at closing.
RDA
Redevelopment Agency -- a city tool using tax increment financing to subsidize land costs for workforce housing.
Silent Second Lien
A 0% subordinated loan that reduces the buyer's first mortgage without increasing monthly payments to a servicer.
