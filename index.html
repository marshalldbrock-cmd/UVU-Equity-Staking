Our Mission
H4U (HomeOwnership4U) exists to close the homeownership gap for Utah County working families. We partner directly with cities to apply a proven, data-driven playbook that removes unnecessary cost from the development process -- so a family earning $100K--$150K can actually afford to buy a home in the community where they work.
